Naming and Notation:

    File names are of the form 'MMDDYYYY_HHMM.txt' (where HH is the hour and MM is the minute)

    Files should contain details of meeting discussion topics and things of note. 

    Essential items should be marked with '*', and non-essential but perhaps useful items marked with '->'
